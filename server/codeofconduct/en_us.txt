Welcome to Cabal SMP!

Please follow this Code of Conduct:

1. Be respectful. No harassment, hate speech, bullying, or slurs.
2. No griefing, stealing, or scamming.
3. No cheating, hacks, x-ray, dupes, exploits, or automation that gives unfair advantage.
4. No spam in chat, signs, or books.
5. Keep builds and redstone reasonable; avoid lag machines.
6. Respect claims, bases, and community projects.
7. No NSFW, doxxing, threats, or illegal content.
8. Follow moderator instructions and server announcements.
9. Report serious issues or bugs to staff instead of abusing them.
10. Have fun and help keep the server welcoming.

Failure to follow these rules may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, bans, or rollbacks.
